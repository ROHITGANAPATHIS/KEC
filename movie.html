Top 5 Movies

A handpicked collection of films that left a deep impact on audiences — from global blockbusters to Tamil cinematic masterpieces.

Spider-Man
Released: 2014
Director: Marc Webb
Cast: Andrew garfield, Emma stone
Summary: When Peter Parker’s identity is revealed, he turns to Doctor Strange for help—leading to a multiverse collision with past Spider-Men.
Thalapathi
Released: 1991
Director: Mani Ratnam
Cast: Rajinikanth, Mammootty, Arvind Swamy
Summary: A modern retelling of Karna’s story from Mahabharata, blending action, friendship, and sacrifice in a gripping underworld setting.
Sita Ramam
Released: 2022
Director: Hanu Raghavapudi
Cast: Dulquer Salmaan, Mrunal Thakur, Rashmika Mandanna
Summary: A poetic love story told through lost letters between an army officer and a mysterious woman named Sita — crossing time and borders.
Nayakan
Released: 1987
Director: Mani Ratnam
Cast: Kamal Haasan, Saranya
Summary: Loosely inspired by The Godfather, this crime drama tells the story of Velu Naicker, a don revered and feared in Bombay’s slums.
Anbe Sivam
Released: 2003
Director: Sundar C.
Cast: Kamal Haasan, R. Madhavan
Summary: A touching journey of two strangers with opposing ideologies who discover the meaning of love, life, and God through each other.
